About

I got into automation
because I hate wasted effort.

I grew up watching smart, capable people burn hours on tasks that a well-designed system would handle in seconds. Chasing leads that went cold. Repeating the same call with the same pitch. Wondering why nobody replied.

That’s what drew me to HubSpot and marketing automation — not the technology, but what it makes possible. When a system is built right, people show up to conversations that are already warm. Sales reps walk in informed. Leads feel understood before they’ve spoken to anyone.

I’m a CRM and lifecycle specialist based in Bournemouth, UK. MBA-qualified, 13+ HubSpot certifications, and genuinely curious about what’s broken in your pipeline.
